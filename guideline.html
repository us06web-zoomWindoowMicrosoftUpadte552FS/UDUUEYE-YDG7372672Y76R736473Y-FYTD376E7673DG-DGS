If something doesn't work

Common hiccups and quick fixes

!

Nothing happens when you double-click the file

Make sure the download finished completely (check your browser's download bar). Try right-clicking the file and choosing Open.

!

"This app can't run on your PC" message

Your download may not match your system (32-bit vs 64-bit). Re-download from the official source and pick the version matching your device.

!

Camera, mic, or screen share not working after install

Check your operating system's privacy settings to confirm the app has been granted permission to use these.
